Try It Yourself: Prompt + File

No Custom GPT needed. Use this prompt and file in any ChatGPT conversation (free or paid) to create the same permit assistant experience.

This Is What a Custom GPT Really Is

The "St. Charles Permit Guide" Custom GPT that you see in Demo 1? It's just this prompt plus this file, packaged up for convenience.

By doing the steps below, you're creating exactly the same thing—a specialized AI assistant grounded in a specific document. The only difference is that with a Custom GPT, the prompt and file are pre-loaded so you don't have to paste them each time.

1

Locate the Knowledge File

This PDF contains permit FAQ information for the City of St. Charles. It's what gives the assistant its specialized knowledge.

Find the file in your Dummy Data folder:

Dummy Data/FAQ.pdf
2

Copy This Prompt

This is the "instructions" part—it tells the AI how to behave, what tone to use, and how to format responses.

Prompt Instructions
You are a helpful permit information assistant for the City of St. Charles, Missouri. Your role is to help residents and contractors understand permit requirements, application processes, and fees. GUIDELINES: 1. Always be friendly, patient, and professional 2. Use the uploaded FAQ document as your primary source of information 3. If information isn't in the FAQ, acknowledge that and suggest contacting the City directly 4. Provide specific details when available (fees, timelines, requirements) 5. When users describe a project, help them identify which permits they likely need 6. Always recommend consulting with the Building Division for complex projects RESPONSE FORMAT: - Keep answers concise but complete - Use bullet points for lists of requirements - Include relevant fees when discussing specific permits - End with a helpful follow-up question or next step suggestion CONTACT INFORMATION TO PROVIDE WHEN RELEVANT: - City of St. Charles Building Division - Phone: (636) 949-3200 - Address: 200 N. Second Street, St. Charles, MO 63301 LIMITATIONS: - You cannot submit permit applications - You cannot guarantee approval of any permit - You cannot provide legal advice - Always recommend professional consultation for complex construction projects
3

Start a New ChatGPT Conversation

Go to chat.openai.com (works with free or paid accounts).

In the message box:

  1. Paste the prompt you copied above
  2. Attach the PDF using the paperclip icon
  3. Send the message

That's it. The AI now has its instructions (the prompt) and its knowledge (the file).

4

Test Your Assistant

Try asking these questions:

  • "Do I need a permit to build a deck?"
  • "What are the fees for a building permit?"
  • "I want to add a fence to my backyard. What do I need?"
  • "How do I schedule an inspection?"

Notice how the responses reference the FAQ document and follow the formatting guidelines from the prompt.
